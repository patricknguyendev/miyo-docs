The debt workspace helps you organize debt details and explore payoff scenarios. It does not move money or make payments for you.

## What you can track

- Current balance and APR
- Minimum payment and due day
- Promotional APR details
- Payment history, including principal, interest, and fees
- Balance corrections and snapshots

## Compare payoff strategies

Miyo can model approaches such as avalanche, snowball, hybrid, utilization-focused, and custom ordering. A projection depends on the balances, rates, minimums, and extra amount you provide.

<Info>
  A payoff projection is an estimate, not a lender statement or financial recommendation. Interest rules, fees, rate changes, and posting dates can produce different results.
</Info>

## Keep the workspace accurate

1. Compare balances with lender statements.
2. Log payments using the principal, interest, and fee amounts when available.
3. Correct an inaccurate balance rather than creating a duplicate debt.
4. Re-run the projection after a material rate or payment change.

<Warning>
  Miyo does not currently rely on Plaid Liabilities to populate the debt workspace. Verify debt details directly with the lender.
</Warning>
