Different controls have different effects. Choose the narrowest action that matches what you want to change.

| Action | Stops future bank sync | Removes imported transactions | Removes your Miyo access |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Disconnect institution | Yes | Not by itself | No |
| Reset linked-institution data, when offered | Already disconnected | Removes the selected local institution data | No |
| Delete an individual transaction | No | Removes that transaction from normal views | No |
| Delete Miyo account | Yes | Deactivates access and starts the account-deletion process | Yes |

## Export transactions

Open **Transactions** and select **Export CSV** to download transactions you own. Store the export securely because it can contain sensitive descriptions, amounts, dates, categories, and notes.

## Disconnect a bank

Use **Settings → Linked accounts → Disconnect institution** to revoke the live Plaid connection and stop future refreshes. Previously imported records can remain until you remove the local data or complete account deletion.

## Delete your Miyo account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Export anything you need">
    Download your transaction export and note any planning information you need to retain outside Miyo.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review household impact">
    If you own a household, review the remaining member and shared information before continuing.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the deletion dialog">
    Open **Settings → Security**, choose **Delete Miyo account**, and read every consequence.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Type DELETE">
    Enter `DELETE` exactly, then select **Delete account**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Deleting immediately deactivates the account, revokes sessions, disables app access, and removes live connected-account access. Miyo can retain limited records when required for audit, security, legal, tax, backup, fraud-prevention, or compliance purposes.

<Warning>
  Account deletion cannot be undone from the app. Contact [privacy@joinmiyo.com](mailto:privacy@joinmiyo.com) before deleting if you need a data-access, correction, export, or specific deletion request.
</Warning>

Read the current [Miyo Privacy Policy](https://joinmiyo.com/privacy) for retention and rights details.
