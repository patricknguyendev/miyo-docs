## Product support

Email [support@joinmiyo.com](mailto:support@joinmiyo.com) for help with accounts, transactions, budgets, goals, household setup, billing, or the dashboard.

Include the page, approximate time, what you expected, and what occurred. Screenshots are helpful after you remove balances, account identifiers, and personal details that are not needed.

## Privacy

Email [privacy@joinmiyo.com](mailto:privacy@joinmiyo.com) for privacy questions or requests involving your personal information.

## Security

Email [security@joinmiyo.com](mailto:security@joinmiyo.com) to report suspected unauthorized access or a security vulnerability.

<Warning>
  Never send a password, one-time verification code, authenticator secret, recovery code, Plaid credential, access token, full bank account number, or complete financial export by email.
</Warning>
