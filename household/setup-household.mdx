---
title: "Set up a household"
description: "Invite another person and prepare Miyo for shared money management."
keywords: ["household", "partner", "invite", "sharing"]
---

A household lets members coordinate around the financial information they intentionally share. Joining a household does not automatically make every account visible.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Choose shared use">
    Review your relationship settings and select the option to use Miyo with a partner or household.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create or review the household">
    Open **Household** from the sidebar. Confirm the household name and your current member list.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send an invitation">
    Invite the other person using the email address they will use for Miyo. Pending invitations must be accepted before the person becomes an active member.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review visibility">
    Open **Privacy** and choose a visibility state for each account. Use the partner-view preview before you rely on shared totals.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Agree on a routine">
    Use **Money Date** as a recurring check-in for reviewing changes and deciding what needs attention together.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Only invite someone you trust with the information you choose to share. Review account visibility before and after the invitation is accepted.
</Warning>
