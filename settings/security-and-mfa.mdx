Your Miyo account can contain sensitive financial information. Protect the email account and devices you use to access it as carefully as Miyo itself.

## Security checklist

- Use a unique password that you do not reuse on another service.
- Enable multi-factor authentication from your security settings when available.
- Store recovery codes somewhere separate from the device you normally use.
- Sign out on shared devices.
- Review unexpected verification or password-reset messages before acting.

## Multi-factor authentication

Miyo supports additional verification flows, including authenticator-based setup. Follow the enrollment instructions in **Settings**, then confirm that a recovery method is available before leaving the page.

<Warning>
  Miyo support will not ask for your password, authenticator secret, one-time verification code, recovery code, Plaid credential, or full bank account number.
</Warning>

## If you suspect unauthorized access

1. Change your Miyo password from a trusted device.
2. Secure the email account connected to Miyo.
3. Review household membership and account visibility.
4. Contact [security@joinmiyo.com](mailto:security@joinmiyo.com) with a description of what you observed.

Do not include secrets or complete financial account numbers in the report.
