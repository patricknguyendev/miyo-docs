---
title: "Manage notifications"
description: "Manage Miyo notification categories and delivery channels for in-app, email, and push alerts, and troubleshoot missing account or budget messages."
---

Notifications can call attention to account, security, budget, transaction, and product events. Available delivery methods can include in-app, email, or push notifications.

## Review preferences

Open notification settings to enable or disable the categories and channels available to your account. Security and service messages may not follow the same opt-out rules as optional product updates.

## If notifications are missing

- Confirm that the relevant category is enabled in Miyo.
- Check browser or device notification permissions for push delivery.
- Review spam or filtered folders for email delivery.
- Confirm that your profile email address is current and verified.

<Note>
  A notification is a prompt to review Miyo, not a substitute for an institution alert or account statement.
</Note>
