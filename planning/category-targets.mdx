title: "Create or update a category target"
description: "Create, edit, pause, or remove a Miyo budget category target and choose the schedule that controls how much the category should receive."

---

A category target turns a budget amount into a repeatable instruction. Use one when you want Miyo to calculate how much a category needs this period or by a specific date.

<Info>
  Category targets can be enabled gradually. If **Set budget target** does not appear for a category, continue entering the monthly budget amount directly.
</Info>

## Set the target

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open a category">
    In **Plan your money → Monthly budget**, open the category's actions and choose **Set budget target**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter the target amount">
    Enter a positive amount. Its meaning depends on the cadence you choose next.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a cadence">
    Select **Weekly**, **Monthly**, **Yearly**, or **By a date**.

    For a repeating cadence, the amount applies to each week, month, or year. For **By a date**, it is the total available balance you want by that date.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose how leftovers behave">
    For a repeating target, choose **Add another** or **Refill available**. See [Choose a funding style](/planning/funding-styles) for examples.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save the target">
    Select **Set target**. Miyo shows the category's progress against the instruction.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Choose a cadence

| Cadence | The amount means | Example use |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Weekly** | Amount for each week | Groceries or commuting |
| **Monthly** | Amount for each month | Dining or household supplies |
| **Yearly** | Amount for each year | Annual fees or holiday spending |
| **By a date** | Total balance to reach by one date | Insurance renewal or planned trip |

<Warning>
  A \$1,200 **Yearly** target means \$1,200 for each year. Use **By a date** when you mean one \$1,200 finish line on a specific date.
</Warning>

## Edit or remove a target

Open the category target again. Select **Save target** after changing it, or **Remove target** to return the category to a manually planned amount.
