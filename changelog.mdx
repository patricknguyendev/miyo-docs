<Update label="July 13, 2026" tags={["Launch"]}>

## Miyo Help Center is here

The new Miyo Help Center is live with practical, step-by-step guides for managing money on your own or with your household.

### New

- **[Getting started guides](/getting-started/quickstart)** — Set up your Miyo account, connect your first accounts, and learn how to navigate the app.
- **[Accounts and transactions](/accounts/connect-and-manage-accounts)** — Connect institutions, manage transactions, and [import history](/transactions/import-transactions) from other tools.
- **[Budgets and category targets](/budgets/overview)** — [Pick a budget mode](/budgets/choose-budget-mode), [build your monthly plan](/budgets/create-and-manage-budget), and set [category targets](/budgets/category-targets) that keep spending on track.
- **[Savings goals](/goals/create-savings-goal)** — Plan for longer-term goals separately from your monthly budget.
- **[Recurring review](/recurring/review-recurring)** and **[debt workspace](/debt/debt-workspace)** — Stay ahead of subscriptions, bills, and payoff plans.
- **[Household and privacy](/household/setup-household)** — Share a household, control [account visibility](/household/account-visibility), run a [money date](/household/money-date), and manage what's shared in the [Privacy Center](/privacy/privacy-center).
- **[Settings and support](/settings/security-and-mfa)** — Configure [security and MFA](/settings/security-and-mfa), tune [notifications](/settings/notifications), and reach us through [troubleshooting](/support/troubleshooting), [FAQ](/support/faq), or [contact](/support/contact).

</Update>
