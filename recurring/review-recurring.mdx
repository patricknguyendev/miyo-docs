---
title: "Review recurring charges"
description: "Understand recurring activity detected from your transaction history."
---

Miyo looks for repeating patterns in your transaction history to help you anticipate charges. The **Recurring** page groups upcoming and recurring activity into a calendar-oriented view.

## What detection uses

Recurring detection is based on transaction history available to Miyo. It is not a guarantee that a merchant will charge the same amount or date again.

## Improve the result

- Keep connected accounts syncing or import complete manual-account history.
- Correct transaction descriptions and categories when needed.
- Review new charges before treating an estimate as a confirmed bill.
- Compare upcoming charges with the merchant or institution when timing matters.

<Warning>
  A recurring item in Miyo does not cancel, pause, or modify a subscription with the merchant. Contact the merchant directly to change the underlying service.
</Warning>

## Scheduled transactions

Some accounts may also have access to scheduled transaction features. Scheduled entries are user-defined plans; recurring items are patterns inferred from past activity. They serve different purposes.
