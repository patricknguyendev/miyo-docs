Miyo offers two ways to plan spending. Both use your categories, but they answer different questions.

<Columns cols={2}>
  <Card title="Simple" icon="gauge">
    Set monthly category limits and compare spending against them. Choose this when you want a lighter planning routine.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Advanced" icon="layers-3">
    Assign money to envelopes that roll over, organize groups, and create savings envelopes. Choose this when you want to track what remains available.
  </Card>
</Columns>

## How to decide

| If you want to… | Choose |
| --- | --- |
| Check whether spending stays below a monthly limit | **Simple** |
| Carry unused money into a future month | **Advanced** |
| Group related envelopes and move money between them | **Advanced** |
| Start planning with minimal setup | **Simple** |

Open **Budget**, choose **Monthly budget**, then use the **Simple** or **Advanced** control. You can also review the mode from relationship settings.

<Warning>
  Switching modes changes how the Budget page represents planned and available money. Review your categories and totals after a change before making financial decisions from the new view.
</Warning>
