The Privacy Center is the control point for household account visibility. It shows counts for shared, private, and hidden accounts without requiring you to open every account first.

## Account visibility list

Each row shows the account, current balance, visibility state, and a short explanation. Change the state only after considering how the account should affect shared views.

## Partner-view preview

Use the preview to inspect the household-facing result before assuming another member sees the same totals or records you do.

## Privacy audit log

The audit log records account visibility changes with the account name, previous state, new state, and time of change.

<Tip>
  Review the Privacy Center after connecting a new institution, accepting a household invitation, or changing an account's purpose.
</Tip>

<Warning>
  Screenshots and exports you share outside Miyo are no longer controlled by account visibility settings. Review them separately before sending.
</Warning>
